Welcome to One Cainta College

One Cainta College is a government higher education institution established by the Local Government Unit of Cainta, Rizal. Founded in 2017, our goal is to provide accessible, affordable, and quality education to deserving students, especially residents of Cainta and nearby municipalities.

We are committed to academic excellence, character formation, and community service. All our programs are recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED), ensuring our graduates are competent and competitive in their chosen fields.

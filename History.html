School History

One Cainta College was created through the initiative of the Municipal Government of Cainta to address the growing demand for affordable higher education in the area. It was formally established and opened to students in 2017.

Starting with a limited number of courses and facilities, the college has continuously expanded over the years. With the strong support of the LGU and compliance with CHED standards, it now offers several degree programs, employs qualified faculty, and provides a conducive learning environment. Today, it stands as a premier public college in Rizal, serving hundreds of students every year.

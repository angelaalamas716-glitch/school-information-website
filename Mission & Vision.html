Mission & Vision

Vision

One Cainta College (OCC) is a recognized reputable educational institution of higher learning committed to nurture and elevate the quality of the lives of its people through accessible quality education that promotes excellence in instruction, research, community development, and partnerships.

Mission

The mission of the OCC is to provide high-quality educational programs, services, and support to the students of Cainta to prepare them for career success in the global economy. It shall offer quality and relevant non-degree and undergraduate degree programs that are based on community and students' needs to lead them to better lives. It shall engage in transformational development as a college to address societal needs towards positive development of the social, economic, and cultural life of the people of Cainta and the global society.

Core Values

The college is guided by a set of core values:

  • O – Openness
  • N – Nurture relationships
  • E – Ethical and professional behavior
  • C – Creativity and Independent thinking
  • A – Active engagement
  • I – Integrity
  • N – Nationalism
  • T – Teamwork
  • A – Accountability and honesty

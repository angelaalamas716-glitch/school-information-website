Frequently Asked Questions

Is tuition free at One Cainta College?

Tuition is highly subsidized. Qualified students can avail of free tuition under the Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act (UniFAST Law) and other government scholarship programs.

Are the programs recognized?

Yes, all courses offered are fully recognized and accredited by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).

What are the admission requirements?

Requirements include: Form 137/138, PSA Birth Certificate, Certificate of Good Moral Character, Entrance Exam Result, and two (2) copies of 2x2 ID photos.

Can non-residents of Cainta enroll?

Yes, the college accepts students from neighboring towns and cities, subject to available slots and admission requirements.

Do you offer online classes?

Currently, classes are conducted face-to-face, with flexible learning options available as needed.
